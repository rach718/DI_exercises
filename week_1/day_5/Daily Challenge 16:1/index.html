TIBERIUS

Tiberias is an Israeli city on the western shore of the Sea of Galilee. Its Old City holds important Jewish and Christian pilgrimage sites including the Tomb of Maimonides and Abulafia (Etz Chaim) Synagogue.

The waterfront and south of the city have exciting sites worth exploring!

TZFAT New

Safed is a city in the Northern District of Israel. Located at an elevation of 900 metres, Safed is the highest city in the Galilee and in Israel.

Due to its high elevation, Safed experiences warm summers and cold, often snowy, winters. Explore ancient sites in this quaint hilltop city.
